Serve with Honor - Join Indian Army

How to Join

NDA

For 12th pass students through the National Defence Academy (training in Khadakwasla, Pune).

CDS

Graduates can apply via UPSC’s Combined Defence Services exam for officer entries.

Technical Entries

Engineering graduates via SSC Tech & TGC entries in specialized fields.

Medical Corps

Doctors and healthcare professionals serve through the Army Medical Corps.

Facts & Strength

Troop Strength

1.4 million active personnel & 2.1 million reserves.
[Source: Wikipedia]

Camps & Divisions

7 Commands, 14 Corps, 37 Divisions, 40+ training academies & camps nationwide.

Wars & Operations

1947, 1965, 1971 wars won; 1999 Kargil victory. Countless peacekeeping missions worldwide.

Missile Arsenal

BrahMos, Agni, Prithvi, Akash, Nag – indigenously developed defense missiles.

Respect & Honors

Param Vir Chakra, Maha Vir Chakra & Vir Chakra awarded to brave warriors.
